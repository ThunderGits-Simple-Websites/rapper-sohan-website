About Sohan

The Voice of a Generation

Born in the Sitamarhi of the city, Sohan's journey from local battles to international stardom has been nothing short of legendary. With his unique flow and thought-provoking lyrics, he's carved a niche for himself in the competitive world of hip-hop.

His music transcends conventional boundaries, blending elements of traditional hip-hop with modern production techniques, creating a sound that's uniquely his own. Sohan's songs often address social issues, personal struggles, and triumphs, resonating with millions of fans worldwide.
